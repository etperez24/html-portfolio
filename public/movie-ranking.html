The Best Movies According to Ephraim

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Mean Girls (2004)

Iconic classic all about the color pink, boys and girl-world drama, that spawned a pop culture movement and countless memes.

Scream (1996)

The horror movie of the late 90's that subverted expectations and took the genre in a new direction.

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: The Movie (1995)

A campy kids movie, with a dark hero design in the esque of the Batman movies from the 80's and 90's.

Crossroads (2002)

Starring pop princess Britney Spears, tells a journey of a woman finding her way in the world.

Pretty Guardian Sailor Moon Cosmos (2023)

A tragic and heartwearming 2-part experience for the 5th arc of Sailor Moon, concluding the teenage journey of Usagi Tsukino.
